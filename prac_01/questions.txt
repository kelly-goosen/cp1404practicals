Problem 1
Nouns: Customer name; date of booking; unique booking id; hourly charge; number of hours; total charge
Verbs: Calculate; use; make; entered; displayed

get customer_name
get booking_date
get hourly_charge
get stay_duration_hr
booking_id = booking_date + customer_name
total_charge = hourly_charge * stay_duration_hr

Problem 2

Verbs: Ask the user; user will be shown

get time_min
time_hours = time_min / 60
average_speed_kmh = distance_km / time_hours
